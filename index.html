About the Word Unscrambler

This free word unscrambler helps you find every valid English word hidden inside a set of jumbled letters. Simply type your letters, choose a minimum word length, and get instant results sorted by length - handy for Scrabble, Words With Friends, Wordle, crosswords, and other word games. The tool checks your letters against a dictionary of over 350,000 English words entirely inside your browser, so nothing you type is ever sent to a server.
